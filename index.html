About PAF-IAST

A Vision for Technical Excellence in Pakistan

The Pak-Austria Fachhochschule: Institute of Applied Sciences and Technology (PAF-IAST) is at the fulcrum of the rapidly evolving global job market for highly skilled human resources, transforming Pakistan's youth into a national dividend.

It is an initiative of the Government of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa in collaboration with Austrian and Chinese universities. Our aim is to raise the quality of technical education in Pakistan through skill training and human resource development.

  • Purpose-built campus with state-of-the-art smart classrooms & labs
  • International collaborations with Austrian and Chinese universities
  • Mandatory internship every semester for practical industry exposure
  • Need and merit-based local and foreign scholarships available
  • Research grants of USD 50,000 per faculty through Technology Park
